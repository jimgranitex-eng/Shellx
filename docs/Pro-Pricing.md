# ShellX Pro — Pricing & Plans

ShellX Pro is the commercial edition of the ShellX Cognitive Developer Engine.  
It includes advanced modules, enterprise-grade diagnostics, and premium support.

---

## 🥇 Plans

### Starter — $10/month

Perfect for solo developers.

Includes:
- ShellX Pro core engine
- Superman Pro (enhanced diagnostics)
- Advanced multi-agent workers
- Priority bug fixes
- Access to private Pro repository

---

### Professional — $25/month

For small teams and power users.

Includes everything in Starter, plus:
- Deep architecture mapping
- Enterprise LinkX timeline
- Stone diff viewer
- VS Code interactive panels
- Early access to V4 modules

---

### Enterprise — $99/month

For companies and production environments.

Includes everything in Professional, plus:
- Unlimited seats
- Custom worker development
- Dedicated support channel
- SLA-backed response times
- Private feature requests
- Deployment consulting
