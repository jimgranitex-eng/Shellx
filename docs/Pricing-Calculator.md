## How to Choose Your Plan

### Solo Developer
→ **Starter — $10/month**

You want:
- Superman Pro for deep diagnostics
- Advanced workers for automation
- Access to the private Pro repository

---

### Small Team (2–5 developers)
→ **Professional — $25/month**

You want:
- Everything in Starter
- Deep architecture mapping
- Enterprise LinkX timeline
- Stone diff viewer
- VS Code interactive panels

*Note: The Professional plan covers up to 5 developers. Each developer needs their own seat.*

Total for 5 developers: 5 × $25 = **$125/month**

---

### Large Team or Organization
→ **Enterprise — $99/month flat**

You want:
- Unlimited seats
- Dedicated support
- SLA-backed response times
- Custom worker development
- Deployment consulting

**Enterprise is the best value for teams of 5 or more.**

Break-even point: 4 developers on Professional ($100/month) vs Enterprise ($99/month flat).
